About Pokestorie

We are a small team of five friends brought together by one thing: a shared love for Pokémon cards. What began as a simple weekend hobby, quickly turned into a mission to find rare cards, nostalgic favourites and new releases for collectors of all ages.

You can usually find the Pokestorie team travelling across local flea markets. Every card we sell has a story behind it: a traded treasure, a lucky discovery or the missing piece of someone’s dream collection.

Here, you can browse some of Pokemon's best sellers, learn more about card collecting and follow our journey as we grow our community one card at a time.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just getting started, Pokestorie is here to help you discover your next favourite card.
